OUR ACTIVITY

Kaiso is a multidisciplinary studio. The diversity of our designer profiles allows us to vary our activity. As a result, our creations range from traditional graphic design to 3D modeling, including game design and motion design.

OUR MÉTHODOLOGY

We apply the same methodology to all of our projects. We have developed this working system based on our various backgrounds and have continuously adapted it throughout our projects to make it as relevant as possible.

Phase 1

Research and experiments.

Monitoring and iconographic research for the project. The goal is to choose a direction together (via mood boards and inspiration boards) at the end of this phase.

Phase 2

Prototyping and ideation.

Graphic research phase. We propose several concepts and collectively choose a set of them to further develop.

Phase 3

Development and finalization.

We develop the chosen concept and finalize it to ensure it accurately meets your needs. We can also make some adjustments to ensure the final product suits you best.
