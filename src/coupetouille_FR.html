Coupe et Touille, la Ratatouille !

Cut the vegetables in rhythm and make the best ratatouille France has ever known! Help Carlo Bonissima, one of the best French cooks, cut vegetables in rhythm in order to create the best ratatouille of the world! Choose the amount and category of vegetables you need and start making the best rhythmic cuisine you've ever seen!

What is the game about ?

Coupe Et Touille is an arcade rhythm game. The player's objective is to simply get the highest score by cutting at the right time the vegetables that appear on the screen. Given that it's a mobile game, it was mandatory to let the players experience the full game loop in a short amount of time. Thus, the game gets more or less difficult and long depending on the amount of vegetables the player decides to cut.

Artistic direction

The game was made in pixel art. This choice was initially made because of the time constraint we gave ourselves. Furthermore, working with pixel art helped our game designer work relatively fast.

The objective was to create a scene with dim warm lights in order to give a chill and tranquil atmosphere to the kitchen. When it comes to the player, he was created using saturated and bright colors in order to direct the player's eye to him.

Constraints

Coupe Et Touille a été réalisé durant un défi que nous nous étions donné. Le défi consistait à créer un prototype ou un projet en 2 semaines suivant un thème tiré au sort, et cela pour chacun des membres du studio. Le thème tiré au sort était "France - Cuisiner". Cela explique donc les choix du game designer concernant la création du jeu.
